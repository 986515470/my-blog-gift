Live with no excuses and travel with no regrets

There are moments in life when you miss someone so much that you just want to pick them from your dreams and hug them for real! Dream what you want to dream;go where you want to go;be what you want to be,because you have only one life and one chance to do all the things you want to do. May you have enough happiness to make you sweet,enough trials to make you strong,enough sorrow to keep you human,enough hope to make you happy? Always put yourself in others’shoes.If you feel that it hurts you,it probably hurts the other person, too.

The happiest of people don’t necessarily have the best of everything;they just make the most of everything that comes along their way.Happiness lies for those who cry,those who hurt, those who have searched,and those who have tried, for only they can appreciate the importance of people. who have touched their lives.Love begins with a smile,grows with a kiss and ends with a tear.The brightest future will always be based on a forgotten past, you can’t go on well in lifeuntil you let go of your past failures and heartaches.

"Years may wrinkle the skin,but to give up enthusiasm wrinkles the soul. Worry, fear, self-distrust bows the heart and turns the spirit back to dust."

Please send this message to those people who mean something to you,to those who have touched your life in one way or another,to those who make you smile when you really need it,to those that make you see the brighter side of things when you are really down, to those who you want to let them know that you appreciate their friendship.And if you don’t, don’t worry,nothing bad will happen to you,you will just miss out on the opportunity to brighten someone’s day with this message..

I do not know who wrote those words, but I have always liked them as a reminder that the future can be anything we want to make it. We can take the mysterious, hazy future and carve out of it anything that we can imagine, just as a sculptor carves a statue from a shapeless stone..

The spring rain of the year of the ox is full of hope, the dream of the year of the ox is shining, the candy of the year of the ox is auspicious, the affection of the year of the ox is very long, and the blessing of the year of the ox is fragrant. Wish you all the best in your life!

Love a beautiful and kind you, say a seat never abandon words, walk the road of life wind and rain hand in hand, keep a lasting love, open a flower of the end of time! Willing to hand through every year!

Take my heart, send the blue sky, through the air, accompanied by the breeze, to your side, greetings, sincere blessing: good health, everything you want, plain sailing. This season, I miss you most. Let the wind bring you heartfelt blessings, fill your sweet dreams, and wish you a brilliant new year.

  • If I had never met you, if I had never fallen in love with you, if I hadn't firmly believed in you all the way, maybe I wouldn't be who I am now.
  • I want to say too much to you, but I have too little chance to say.
  • One person will be free, two people will be interesting.
  • As long as today is better than yesterday, it is the greatest luck on the way.

Every one of us has to go through a lot of hardships and setbacks on the way of growth, different emotions. At the same time, a small wish will quietly grow up in a corner of our heart, but it will fade with the passage of years, take root again, germinate and record the hope of the year. In the world after a thousand years, we can't wait, but in an instant, all our wishes turn into a petal and melt into the soil, A thousand years later, it can still be a tree swaying in the wind, swaying for a long time.

Happy to march on the road of life, the beautiful life described more brilliant, more substantial, in the vibrant Live happily in the wild. This is the role of the precious spiritual wealth and life motivation brought by the wishes of life. Wish is like a seed planted in the soil of heart; wish is like a boat sailing on the sea of heart; wish is like a seed planted in the soil of heart Like a ball of colored thread, weaving our "heart" world..
